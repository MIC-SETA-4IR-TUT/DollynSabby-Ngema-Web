Our poultry place farm specializes in orgin/free range chicken meat production for customers that simply want quality chicken meat for their business consumption. We also stock a selected brand of free range Broiler feeds that optimizes growth and quality in meat for small farmers.



Our poultry farm store stocks essential broiler/poultry farming equipments. We mostly also provide locally fabricated cages of high standard and quality with 7-10 durability. We have the regular cage which have 96 birds capacity and 128 birds capacity.

Our farmers put animal welfare at the heart of their operations. We compensate them fairly and provide ongoing support.
